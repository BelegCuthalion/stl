Then we remove $B$ by a low rank $\nabla Cut$ on this sequent and $\mathbf{D_0}'$ to get $\p^l \mathcal{S}^2 , \mathcal{T} \Rightarrow \Delta$, which yields the desired sequent after enough applications of $Lc$.
